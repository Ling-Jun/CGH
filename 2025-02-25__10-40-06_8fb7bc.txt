*Update* CanadaBuys Portal Maintenance Rescheduled
Please note that the CanadaBuys website critical upgrade has been rescheduled as follows:
New Maintenance Window:
Friday, April 4, 2025, 20:00 (8:00 PM) EDT to Monday, April 7, 2025, 07:00 (7:00 AM) EDT
